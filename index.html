Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I service my bike?

It's recommended to service your bike every 6-12 months, depending on usage.

What should I check before riding after storage?

Check tire pressure, brakes, chain lubrication, and overall frame condition before riding.

Can I ride my bike in the rain?

Yes, but ensure proper fenders and check braking efficiency as wet conditions reduce grip.
